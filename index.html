I'm one of those guys that just can't stop optimizing things. If you've ever heard of Soylent, I'm that kind of guy. Anything I'm interested in, anything at all; I'll just take it and start figuring out how to make it easier, more efficient, or more effective.

Projects

OpenCommunicate (source) is a clone of Facebook built with Ruby on Rails and Backbone.js.

It includes complex queries such as friends of friends sorted by and including mutual friend count.

Directed Go is a generalization of the popular strategic board game Go playable on arbitrary directed graphs.

It implements Super Ko and extensive use of flood-fill. It is also backwards compatible with Go itself.

The Best Space Game in the World (source) is a top down side-scrolling shoot-em-up bullet-hell space game with a multitude of upgrades. It has a powerful physics/rendering engine and extremely modular code.

It also has absolutely fabulous particle effects. ;)

Rails Lite (source) is a lite version of Rails coded in Ruby which includes ActiveRecord-like base classes and associations.
